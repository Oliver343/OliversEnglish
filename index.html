Using Real English.

The sentence “I play football.” is correct in English, however in a conversation it is more likely we would say “I am going to play football.” We will look into this in more detail here.

Example

I play football.
I am going to play football. Future - Tomorrow / Next Week / Next year
I am playing football. Present - Right now!
I played football. Past - Yesterday / Last week / Last year
